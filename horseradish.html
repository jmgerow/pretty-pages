Roast Beef and Horseradish Sandwich


Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of french bread, cut into the size you'll eat
  • freshly sliced medium rare roast beef
  • 1 Tbsp DiLusso horseradish sauce, more or less as you prefer
  • 2 Tbsp mayonase, more or less as you prefer
  • havarti cheese




Directions:

  1. This recipe is so simple, just take these ingredients and make a sandwich, using any amount you think is the most delicious. The horseradish sauce has a wonderfully strong kick to it, so I prefer to layer a small amount on both top and bottom slices of bread and layer everything else in between.
