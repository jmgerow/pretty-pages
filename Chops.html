Pork Chops with Sage Butter


Ingredients:

  • 2 12-oz pork chops , about 1 inch thick
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper , to taste
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small handful fresh sage leaves
  • 1 small shallot ,sliced
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice


Directions:

  1. Rub olive oil all over pork chops and season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. Warm a cast iron skillet over medium high heat. Add pork chops and sear until golden brown, about 7 minutes.
  3. Flip pork chops and add 1 tablespoon of butter, sage and shallot. Cook until a meat thermometer inserted into the chops registers 135Â°F, about 7 minutes more.
  4. Transfer pork chops to a plate to rest. Add remaining tablespoon of butter and lemon juice to skillet. Taste and add more salt and pepper as needed. Serve butter sauce over pork chops.
