No Bake Cookies


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted Irish butter
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract





Directions:

  1. In a large pot add sugar, milk, butter, and cocoa powder. Slowly bring to a boil while whisking. Once at a rolling boil continuously stir for exactly 3 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and add in oats. Mix well. Add in peanut butter, mix well. Last add in vanilla extract and mix well.
  3. Lay out parchment paper on the counter and spoon out the dough into cookie shapes. Allow to set for 15-30 minutes.
