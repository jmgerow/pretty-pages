Dense Chocolate Cake


Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup unsalted butter softened
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 1 cup baking cocoa sifted


  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup instant chocolate pudding mix small box
  • 2 1/4 cups buttermilk
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

























Directions:

  1. Preheat regular, conventional oven to 350°.
  2. Beat sugar, butter & vanilla in large bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs.
  4. Mix in cake flour, baking cocoa, soda, salt, chocolate pudding and buttermilk.
  5. Stir in semi-sweet chocolate chips.
  6. Pour into greased 2 8-inch round pans.
  7. Bake in regular, conventional oven at 350° for 30 minutes.
  8. Adjust baking temperature down to 325° and continue baking 30-40 minutes more, until cake tester (toothpick) comes out clean.
  9. Cool cake completely before icing.

      10. note: This cake will pare perfectly with this buttercream frosting recipe.
