Baked Mostaccioli


Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces mostaccioli noodles
  • 1 1/2 lbs ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can Tomato paste
  • 1 (28 ounce) can Tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can Tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Basil, dried


  • 1 Bay leaf
  • 1 (4 ounce) can Mushrooms
  • 1/8 tsp Pepper
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 2 cups Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup Water

















Directions:

  1. Cook mostaccioli according to package directions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, brown ground beef, onion and garlic; drain.
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato sauce and paste, mushrooms, water, salt, sugar, basil, pepper and bay leaf. Bring to a boil; reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove bay leaf. Stir in mostaccioli.
  4. Spoon half into a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese; layer with remaining meat mixture. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil.
  5. Bake at 350 Â° for 30-35 minutes or until heated through.
  6. Let stand 5 minutes before serving
