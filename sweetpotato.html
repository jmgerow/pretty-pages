Sweet Potato Casserole


Ingredients:

  • 1 large can of sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup mini marshmallows (more or less as desired)
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup Irish butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt


  • 2 cloves, ground
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp cardamom
  • 1 Tbsp ground ginger














Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Butter a 2 quart casserole dish and set aside.
  2. Combine all ingredients in a loarge bowl and then mash with a potato masher or hand mixer until well mixed.
  3. Pour ingredients into the casserole dish and bake for 45 minutes.
  4. After 45 minutes sprinkle the marshmallows over the mix and broil on low for 10 minutes or until marshmallows are a golden toasted color.
