Creamy Tuscan Chicken


Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken thighs, cleaned and dried
  • 4 tsp salt (2 tsp for the chicken rub 2 tsp for the sauce)
  • 2 tsp pepper (1
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 Tbsp butter


  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 of an onion, minced
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 2 cups fresh spinach, rinsed and dried
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup parmesan



















Directions:

  1. Heat up skillet and oil over medium heat and preheat your oven to 375 degrees.
  2. While your skillet is heating up, place the chicken in a bowl and rub in 2 tsp salt and 1 tsp pepper.
  3. Put butter into skillet. Once the butter melts put in the chicken, skin side down. Cook 7 minutes and then flip, cooking the other side for 5 minutes.
  4. Take the chicken out of skillet and place on a clean baking sheet. Bake chicken for 10 minutes while you make the sauce.
  5. Add the garlic and onion to the skillet and cook for about 3 minutes, constantly stirring.
  6. Add tomato and spinach and stir until the spinach has cooked down.
  7. Pour in the heavy cream
  8. Add 2 Tsp salt and 1 tsp pepper, stir and allow the sauce to thicken for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Place the chicken back in the sauce and spoon sauce over the chicken. Let chicken sit in the sauce for 2 minutes before serving.
