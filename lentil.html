Lentil Soup


Ingredients:

  • 1 (15 ounce) can Black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 Carrots finely chopped
  • 5 cloves Garlic finely chopped
  • 1 cup Lentils, dried
  • 1 Sweet or yellow onion finely chopped
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can Tomatoes
  • 4 cups Vegetable broth
  • 1/2 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Kosher or sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil, extra-virgin
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin















Directions:

  1. In a large pot add olive oil and sauté garlic for one minute.
  2. Add diced carrots and onion and continue to sauté for about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the remaining ingredients and cover.
  4. Bring to a boil over medium heat, reduce heat to a simmer and cook until lentils and carrots are tender, about 25 to 30 minutes.
