The Best Scrambled Eggs


Feeds 2 people

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1 slice of pepperjack cheese broken into pieces
  • 1/4 tsp of red pepper flakes
  • 1 Tbps salted Irish butter
  • salt and pepper to preference




Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients (except butter) in a small bowl while a nonstick pan is heating up over medium heat.
  2. Once the pan is hot, melt the butter and then the mixed ingredients.
  3. Once the mix starts to firm up begin to stir with a wooden spoon. Keep stirring for about 5 minutes until eggs are cooked through, resembling light and fluffy curds.
  4. Serve with buttered toast.
