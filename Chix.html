Chicken Salad Sandwich


Ingredients:

  • 3 c. cooked chicken breast or rotisserie, pulled
  • 2 celery stalks, cut down the middle and sliced
  • 1/2 apple, chopped
  • 1/3 c. red grapes, cut in halves
  • 1/4 c. plain Greek yogurt or light sour cream (more or less depending on personal preference)


  • 1/2 c. mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp. Dijon mustard
  • 1/8 tsp dill
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/2 c. toasted or candied pecans, chopped (optional)

















Directions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chicken, celery, apple and grapes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek yogurt (or sour cream), mayonnaise, mustard, dill, salt and pepper. Pour the mixture into the bowl and toss to combine. Refrigerate for 1 hour, then fold in the nuts before serving.
